Privacy policy
==============

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for this application/extension.
This privacy policy applies solely to information collected by this software.

What analytics data is collected
================================

1) The print events that a user generates, this EXCLUDES information about the contents of the printed document  - no personal documents are shared with us or any 3rd party.
2) Other information about the startup, running and update of the application/extension.
3) Information about the printer and state of the printer

The information is reported using a randomly generated ID that represents the installation of this software on a particular device. No personally identifiable information is collected.

The IPP/CUPS Printing for G-Suite Admins extension (any only this extension) reports email address to allow per-user print statistics for administrators. Email addresses are encrypted prior to transmission and storage.

Opt-out
=======

If you as an individual or organization have decided to opt out of stats collection, no analytics data is collected by us.

How it is used
==============

a) To analyze the behavior of the application/extension
b) To understand the popularity of aspects of the application/extension.
c) To allow us to fund the development of the application through providing targeted advertising of relevant products via 3rd parties.

Sharing of data
===============

i) We reserve the right to share/sell any anonymised data with 3rd parties.

Updates
========

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time and all updates will be notified on this page.

If you feel that we are not abiding by this privacy policy, you should contact us immediately via email help@directprint.io
